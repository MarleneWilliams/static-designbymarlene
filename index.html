Hi, my name is Marlene Williams. I contract to do front-end web development + build websites using Drupal.

What I do

I have worked with Drupal since 2007 and have built sites using Drupal 5, 6, and 7. I know how to install and configure Drupal to serve a wide variety of functions. Since I'm not a PHP developer and don't write my own functionality, I have become proficient at selecting and using contributed modules.

My favorite website-building tasks, however, involve theming, which is writing and manipulating the code that gives a site its look and feel. A big part of that is responsive web design, making websites that respond well to the wide variety of modern screen sizes. I enjoy building sites that look good now--on your desktop as well as your phone--and that will be compatible with future technologies.

I work well with people, on teams as well as directly with clients. As an independent contractor, I have experience taking a website from the planning stages, through development, and into training and support.
